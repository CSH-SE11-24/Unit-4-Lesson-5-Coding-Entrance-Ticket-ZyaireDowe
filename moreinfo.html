About Tanuki

The tanuki, or raccoon dog, is a species of canid native to East Asia. They are not related to raccoons but earned the name "raccoon dog" due to their similar appearance. Tanuki are known for their distinctive facial markings and are a common subject in Japanese folklore.

These animals are medium-sized and have a thick fur coat. Tanuki are omnivores and have a varied diet that includes fruits, insects, small mammals, and plants.
